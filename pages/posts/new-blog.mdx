After lots of time spent messing around, I've finally finished my blog and I'm very happy with it. Let me walk you through it.

### Past Work

My blog has gone through multiple unseen iterations. It has always been a fun side project for me where I've been able to experiment with new technologies. The issue with that is that I've never had the chance to actually finish a blog engine before writing a post! There was always a new technology that I wanted to try out. So to simplify, I've taken all of the crazy new technology out and am writing this post in markdown and delivering it via [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) and [Vercel](https://vercel.com/).
